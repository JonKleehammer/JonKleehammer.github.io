About Me

Hi everyone!
My name is Jon Kleehammer and I'm a senior at UNT graduating Spring 2020!
I have so many passions, but my greatest include:

Data Gathering and Visualization!
There's something super satisfying about utilizing the huge amount of data in the world and drawing conclusions based on charts and graphs that tell a story. Although I don't have many projects that explore this, it is my ultimate goal to use these skills in industry as a data scientist in the next 3-5 years.

Game Design and Development
In my free time I'm an avid dungeon master for Dungeons and Dragons and game designer using Unity3D.
I see game development as a form of self-expression that I do for fun! I don't see myself doing it as a career, but I really enjoy doing it for myself!

UI/UX Design
Lastly I love designing user interfaces! I am passionate about creating beautiful websites that are easy to use and look awesome.
When I first got to college I was a fine arts major, but I didn't find the work as fulfilling as computer science. After graduating I may get another bachelor's degree in fine arts or graphic design partly for my own enjoyment and partly for improving my skills with making beautiful and functional designs!
